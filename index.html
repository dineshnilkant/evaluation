Faculty Recruitment Evaluator for Colleges, Business School and Universities

Dear Friends

This faculty recruitment evaluation process is divided into two levels: Evaluation A and Evaluation B.

  • Evaluation A is self-explanatory and assesses qualifications, experience, and research output.
  • Evaluation B requires a demonstration of your teaching abilities through video submission.

This process will help us assess your current level, based on which salaries and job opportunities will be suggested.

Levels of Evaluation:

  • Level A: Very Good
  • Level B: Good
  • Level C: Average
  • Level D: Not Satisfactory

Candidates are judged according to this rubric. Please note, this information is for our internal reference to understand the quality of the candidate. However, there will always be an opportunity to upgrade your level internally.

Evaluation B: Teaching Demonstration

Evaluation B Process

Evaluation B will be conducted by Appoint Teachers. You will be informed of your demo class rating and your final level. Along with this, you will also have access to a general and detailed analysis of your performance.

The current level has been created based on your submitted data. However, the final level will only be shared after Evaluation B. To receive the final level, it is mandatory to upload your demo video, where your name and face must be clearly visible.

Maximum file size: 100MB. Your name and face must be clearly visible in the video.
